A patient describes a symptom before a clinic visit.

The system records the voice and creates a draft intake summary.

The summary is not a diagnosis.

The summary is not a treatment recommendation.

Clinical staff review the draft before it becomes part of the visit workflow.

If the draft misses a warning sign, staff can correct it.

If the draft is unclear, staff can ask a follow up question.

The useful goal is a safer review surface, not autonomous medical decision making.
