This pilot checks mixed technical terms.

Automatic speech recognition, or A S R, turns speech into text.

A large language model, or L L M, can organize text into a draft.

R A G means retrieval augmented generation.

FDA five ten k is a regulatory pathway for some medical devices.

In a clinical workflow, triage means sorting or prioritizing patients.

In Mandarin, triage can also be explained as fen liu.

The model should keep these terms understandable and should not replace them with unrelated words.
