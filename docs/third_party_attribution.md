## Source And Asset Scope

This attribution covers the image-generation skill used to help produce the
report illustrations. The video package records the final scene images as
generated report assets under `public/images/`.

The paper screenshots and figures shown in evidence scenes are separate report
evidence assets. They are listed in `docs/asset_manifest.md` and are displayed
to support the academic discussion of the cited papers.

No statement in the video end card should be read as a legal guarantee. The
purpose of the end card is to preserve clear attribution, identify the adapted
project, and keep the claim-evidence boundary visible for course review.
